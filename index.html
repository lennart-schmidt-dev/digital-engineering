About

Join our student hackathon that is all about digital product development in Big Data
and aims to connect students from inspiring universities and diverse backgrounds through tech.
Our initiative creates a platform for around 100 students from top tier universities, with backgrounds in software development,
design and digital business, to connect with industry leading companies and personalities - as well as with each other.
By facilitating this exchange, we want to support the next tech generation to foster positive impact on the digital community.
