DATA NATIVES 2021

Celebrate the potential of Big Data.

Take part in this year's hackathon and experience exciting speakers and work with ambitious students like yourself in a sophisticated challenge from our partners.

March 12, 4pm to March 14 2021
at XU Exponential University campus, Potsdam

Join our student hackathon that is all about digital product development in Big Data and aims to
connect students from inspiring universities and diverse backgrounds through tech.

Our initiative creates a platform for around 100 students from top tier universities, with backgrounds in software development, design and digital business, to connect with industry leading companies and personalities - as well as with each other.

By facilitating this exchange, we want to support the next tech generation to foster
positive impact on the digital community.

What you can expect

Speeches

We invite renowned speakers to talk about their personal experiences with developing digital products and working in the tech industry. Last year’s speakers included CXOs from Trivago, Project A Ventures, Outfittery and Blinkist. For DATA NATIVES 2021, we have convinced amazing entrepreneurs to join us - which we will announce step by step.

Students

The participants are one of the most essential aspects of unicon. We want to make sure to invite students that have similar interests but are at the same time diverse in their backgrounds and skills. Besides, we will create natural networking opportunities so that it is easy to connect with one another.

Challenge

To combine the diverse skills of our attendees, we will organize a group challenge in which they collaborate for four hours in small teams. The goal is to come up with a solution concept for a specific problem and pitch this to a jury and the other participants Each group consists of software engineers, business people and designers.
