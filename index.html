About

Join our student hackathon that is all about digital product development in Big Data and
aims to connect students from inspiring universities and diverse backgrounds through tech.
Our initiative creates a platform for around 100 students from top tier universities, with backgrounds
in software development, design and digital business, to connect with industry leading companies and personalities
- as well as with each other. By facilitating this exchange, we want to support the next tech generation
to foster positive impact on the digital community.

What You Can Expect.

Speeches

We invite renowned speakers to talk about their personal experiences
with developing digital products and working in the tech industry.
Last year’s speakers included CXOs from Trivago, Project A Ventures, Outfittery and Blinkist.
For DATA NATIVES 2021, we have convinced amazing entrepreneurs to join us
- which we will announce step by step.

Students

The participants are one of the most essential aspects of DATA NATIVES.
We want to make sure to invite students that have similar interests,
but are at the same time diverse in their backgrounds and skills. Besides, we will create
natural networking opportunities so that it is easy to connect with one another.

Challenge

To combine the diverse skills of our attendees, we will organize a group challenge
in which they collaborate for four hours in small teams.
The goal is to come up with a solution concept for a specific problem and pitch this to a jury
and the other participants. Each group consists of software engineers,
business people and designers.
